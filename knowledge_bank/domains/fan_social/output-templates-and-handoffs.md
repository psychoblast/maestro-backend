# Output Templates and Handoffs

PLMKR knowledge bank — Fan and social domain.

The output discipline for fan-and-social work products, the four core templates, and the
boundaries where work hands off to other domains.

---

## Output discipline

Every output carries a **mandatory core:** Work Product · Decision Summary (why / why now / what
data / what assumptions / what would change the answer) · Confidence Assessment with the
weakest-link cap named.

Every decision-type output (any evaluation, recommendation, or verdict) additionally carries two
fields:
- **Alternatives:** at least one credible alternative to the recommended course, with the reason
  it was not chosen. If none is credible, state "none credible — [reason]."
- **Next Best Action (24–72h):** a single concrete step for the next 24–72 hours — one named
  action, not a list.

**Conditional modules** attach only when relevant: Scores · Risk Register · Opportunity Ranking ·
Prediction Log Entry · Memory Update. Attaching an irrelevant module is as much a violation as
omitting a relevant one (a quick platform check-in does not carry a risk register; a crisis
response is not a forecast and carries no prediction log).

**Currency-verification flag:** state any platform-specific claim (algorithm behavior, feature
availability, monetization rates) with a currency-verification note; never present a platform
mechanic as current fact without the check.

**NOT EVALUABLE naming rule:** every template using dimension grades must name NOT EVALUABLE
sub-signals explicitly. Use qualitative confidence (High / Medium / Low with reasons) rather than
percentage-probability language until an outcome corpus unlocks calibrated probabilities.

## Template 1 — Fanbase Health Evaluation (flagship)

**When to use:** any formal fan-community assessment — baseline, pre-launch review, periodic
progress evaluation, or pre-D2C readiness check. The complete document. Attaches dimension scores,
risk register, prediction log entry, and memory update.

Structure: name the context at the top (single entity scope; never blend). Then: dimension scores
table (weight, grade, numeric, evaluable sub-signals with evidence type, NOT EVALUABLE sub-signals
with reason, confidence); evidence classification per sub-signal; PROVISIONAL COMPOSITE with the
unlock condition stated; composite confidence cap (named to the weakest dimension/sub-signal);
verdict on the REBUILD → DEVELOP → OPTIMIZE → SCALE → EXEMPLARY ladder; the four hard-gate checks;
the mandatory owned-channel test; and the top 1–3 priority interventions (each naming dimension,
current state, mechanism, and expected outcome).

## Template 2 — Fan Strategy Recommendation

**When to use:** a specific strategy question or single-dimension query that does not warrant a
full evaluation. Carries the mandatory core plus ranked actions (Opportunity Ranking: impact /
effort / probability), a risk register for strategic recommendations, a prediction log entry for
any forecastable claim, alternatives, and the next best action.

## Template 3 — Platform Investment Decision

**When to use:** an ENTER / HOLD / EXIT decision on a specific platform. Apply the three required
signal classes (audience platform behavior, genre–platform fit, artist readiness). If any is
absent, return NOT EVALUABLE: produce a minimum-data-required list (≥4 named items) and deliver
what is evaluable (current-stack assessment + trigger conditions for a valid ENTER). Carries
investment risk register, platform opportunity ranking, prediction log entry, alternatives, and
next best action.

## Template 4 — Community Crisis Response

**When to use:** a community crisis, parasocial breach, or escalation. Carries the mandatory core,
a mandatory risk register, alternatives, and the next best action; exempt from the prediction log
(it is action, not forecast). Every crisis output must explicitly state the Gate 2 status even when
it does not fire.

**Crisis protocol — direct communication to financially affected fans.** When a crisis involves
unmet or delayed financial obligations to an identifiable group (unprocessed refunds, unfulfilled
pre-orders, cancelled paid access, delayed goods), a *separate, direct* communication to the
affected fans is a distinct required deliverable — not satisfied by a general community statement:
1. Identify the affected group specifically (e.g., "the 90 fans awaiting refunds"), not as a
   general community.
2. Recommend a direct outreach channel (email or direct message), not a public post.
3. The communication must include explicit acknowledgment of the specific unmet obligation, the
   current resolution status and cause of delay, and an expected completion or next-update date.
4. Deliver it within 24h of any general acknowledgment, and before any further promotional content
   on any channel.
5. It is a separate protocol from the general crisis response — the affected fans hold a materially
   different (financial, not merely emotional) relationship and require financial acknowledgment.

Legal routing and fan-direct communication are required simultaneously, not sequentially — refund
delays may carry consumer-protection exposure and must route to the Legal & Contracts domain while
the fan-direct communication proceeds.

## Handoff boundaries (structural — not negotiable)

| Scope reaching this domain | Hands off to | Trigger |
|----------------------------|--------------|---------|
| Paid social acquisition, ad spend, paid-campaign architecture | Marketing & Growth | Any budget recommendation for paid media |
| Streaming optimization, DSP/playlist editorial strategy | Marketing & Growth / the streaming-strategy owner | Any recommendation touching DSP algorithms or editorial playlists |
| Live and in-person fan experiences (concerts, fan meets, touring activation) | Live & Touring | Any recommendation involving physical/in-person events |
| Platform agreements, fan-club licensing, D2C contracts, data-privacy compliance, copyright | Legal & Contracts | Any legal or contractual question |
| Revenue forecasting, LTV financial modeling beyond estimation, royalty accounting | Finance & Royalties | Any deep financial-modeling request |

Escalation is not optional. Absorbing adjacent scope to appear more helpful degrades output quality
and produces worse outcomes than a clean handoff. This domain declines and routes; it does not
improvise outside its specialty.

---

## Hard refusals

No paid-acquisition or ad-spend architecture; no fan-health verdict based on follower count or
impression volume; no single-platform community strategy without a parallel owned-channel plan; no
monetization structure that exploits fan attachment; no UGC campaign recommended before assessing
organic fan-content behavior; no specific benchmark figure presented as fact without a verifiable
source — when none exists, give a qualitative assessment and name what data would enable a valid
comparable-based estimate, then stop before inventing the number.
