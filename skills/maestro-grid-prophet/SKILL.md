## Platform Strategy 2025/2026 — Global

### TIKTOK — Highest priority for discovery globally
- Hook must land in under 3 seconds
- Save rate and share rate are most important signals
- Artist-native content outperforms polished production
- Available in 150+ countries — but banned or restricted in some markets
- India: TikTok banned — use Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts instead
- USA: Monitor political situation — potential restrictions
- China: Douyin (Chinese version) — separate platform, separate strategy

### INSTAGRAM — Global brand building
- Reels get 3-5x more reach than static posts
- Stories for daily connection, Feed for brand image
- Strong in: USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Nigeria, Brazil, India, Germany
- Particularly important for Latin American markets

### YOUTUBE — Global long-term catalog
- Second largest search engine globally
- Strong in: India (massive YouTube culture), Southeast Asia, Latin America, Africa
- YouTube Shorts feeding main channel algorithm
- YouTube Music growing in markets where Spotify is weak

### TWITTER/X — Industry and cultural conversations
- Strong in: USA, UK, Japan (massive Twitter culture), Nigeria
- Japan: Twitter/X is dominant social platform — critical for J-Pop and K-Pop

### FACEBOOK — Older demographic, specific markets
- Still dominant in: Southeast Asia (Philippines, Vietnam, Indonesia), parts of Africa
- Facebook Groups strong for community building in these markets

### WEIBO / WECHAT — China specific
- For artists targeting Chinese market
- Requires dedicated China social strategy
- Partner with Chinese digital marketing specialist

### NAVER / KAKAO — South Korea
- Naver Blog and KakaoTalk critical for K-Pop and Korean music
- International K-Pop fans primarily use Twitter/X and Weibo

### BOOMPLAY — Africa specific
- Dominant music streaming and social platform in Africa
- Critical for Nigerian, Ghanaian, Kenyan, and pan-African artists
- Editorial pitching direct to Boomplay team

### LINE — Japan, Thailand, Taiwan
- Messaging and social platform dominant in these markets
- LINE Music: key streaming platform in Japan and Thailand

## Release Campaign Framework — Universal

6 weeks out: Spotify editorial submission (7+ days minimum), Apple Music submission
4 weeks out: Pre-save campaign live, cover art reveal
3 weeks out: Daily content begins, hook snippets on TikTok/Reels
2 weeks out: Blog pitches, independent playlist curator outreach
Release day: Multiple posts all platforms, Live sessions
Week 2-4: Keep content going — algorithm rewards sustained activity

## Paid Advertising by Market

META ADS (Instagram/Facebook):
- Minimum $10/day for meaningful data
- Target by interest, age, location, similar artist fans
- Strongest in: USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Nigeria, Brazil
- Currency note: run ads in local currency of target market

TIKTOK ADS:
- Spark Ads: boost organically performing content only
- Strong in: USA, UK, Southeast Asia, Latin America

YOUTUBE ADS:
- Skippable in-stream ads for music videos
- Strong globally, especially India and Southeast Asia

SPOTIFY MARQUEE AND SHOWCASE:
- Available in: USA, Canada, UK, Australia, Germany, France, and expanding
- $250 minimum for Marquee
