## The Three-Layer Royalty Stack

Every stream of a song written and recorded by an independent artist generates THREE separate royalties:

LAYER 1 — MASTER ROYALTY (~$0.003-0.005/stream)
Paid to: Master recording owner
Via: Distributor (DistroKid, TuneCore, CD Baby, AWAL, etc.)
Most artists collect this — it shows up in distributor dashboard

LAYER 2 — PERFORMANCE ROYALTY (~$0.001-0.002/stream)
Paid to: Songwriter/composer
Via: PRO (SOCAN, ASCAP, BMI, PRS, APRA, etc.)
Requires PRO registration — many artists miss this

LAYER 3 — MECHANICAL ROYALTY (~$0.00091/stream)
Paid to: Songwriter/publisher
Via: MLC (USA), CMRRA (Canada), MCPS (UK), AMCOS (Australia), etc.
Most artists never collect this — least understood royalty stream

## Global Mechanical Societies

### NORTH AMERICA
USA: The MLC — Mechanical Licensing Collective (themlc.com)
- Launched 2021 — collects mechanicals from ALL US streaming services
- Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, YouTube Music, Tidal, Pandora, Deezer
- Free to register
- Critical: had hundreds of millions in unmatched historical royalties at launch
- Register immediately — unmatched royalties get redistributed

CANADA: CMRRA — Canadian Musical Reproduction Rights Agency (cmrra.ca)
- Collects mechanical royalties for Canada
- Physical sales, downloads, some streaming
- Affiliate directly or through SOCAN

### UNITED KINGDOM
MCPS — Mechanical Copyright Protection Society (via PRS for Music)
- Handled through PRS membership
- Covers physical, digital downloads, and streaming mechanicals in UK
- No separate registration needed if already PRS member

### EUROPE
GERMANY: GEMA (gema.de) — handles both performance and mechanical
FRANCE: SDRM (administered by SACEM)
NETHERLANDS: BUMA/STEMRA — STEMRA handles mechanicals
SWEDEN: STIM handles mechanicals
NORWAY: TONO handles mechanicals
SPAIN: SGAE handles mechanicals
ITALY: SIAE handles mechanicals
Most European countries: PRO handles both performance and mechanical

### ASIA PACIFIC
AUSTRALIA: AMCOS (administered by APRA AMCOS)
- Handles mechanical royalties for Australia and NZ
- Included with APRA membership

JAPAN: JASRAC handles mechanicals
SOUTH KOREA: KOMCA handles mechanicals
INDIA: IPRS handles mechanicals

### AFRICA
Most African PROs handle both performance and mechanical royalties:
- Nigeria: COSON
- South Africa: SAMRO/NORM
- Ghana: GHAMRO
- Kenya: MCSK

### LATIN AMERICA
Brazil: UBC/ABPD for mechanicals (separate from ECAD performance)
Mexico: AMPROFON for mechanicals
Argentina: CAPIF for mechanicals

## Royalty Calculations

At 100,000 monthly streams:
- Master royalties: ~$300-500/month (via distributor) ✅ most collect
- Performance royalties: ~$100-200/month (via PRO) ⚠️ many miss
- Mechanical royalties: ~$91/month (via MLC) ❌ most miss
- Annual difference with all three: $2,300-3,500/year per 100K streams
