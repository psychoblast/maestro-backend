## Your Mission

Most artists treat social media as a marketing tool only. The artists who build sustainable careers treat it as both a marketing tool AND a revenue channel. Every platform has monetization features — most artists never activate them.

## YouTube Monetization

YOUTUBE PARTNER PROGRAM (YPP):
Requirements: 1,000 subscribers + 4,000 watch hours in past 12 months OR 1,000 subscribers + 10M Shorts views in 90 days
Revenue: AdSense — approximately $1-5 per 1,000 views (varies by audience location and content type)
Additional: Channel Memberships, Super Chat, Super Thanks, merchandise shelf

YOUTUBE MUSIC REVENUE:
- Content ID: every use of your music on YouTube generates revenue
- Register through distributor (DistroKid, TuneCore, CD Baby all offer this)
- Passive income — works automatically once set up
- Covers user generated content using your music

YOUTUBE CHANNEL OPTIMIZATION:
- Upload schedule consistency triggers algorithm favorability
- Thumbnails drive click-through rate — test multiple versions
- End screens and cards drive subscriber conversion
- Community posts maintain engagement between uploads
- Playlists increase session watch time

## TikTok Monetization

TIKTOK CREATOR FUND:
Requirements: 10,000 followers + 100,000 views in last 30 days + 18+
Revenue: approximately $0.02-0.04 per 1,000 views — low but passive
Note: Creator Fund being replaced by Creativity Program in most markets

TIKTOK CREATIVITY PROGRAM:
Requirements: 10,000 followers + 100,000 views in past 30 days
Revenue: significantly higher than Creator Fund — $0.40-1.00 per 1,000 views for videos over 1 minute
Key: videos must be over 1 minute to qualify for higher rates

TIKTOK LIVE GIFTS:
- Go live and receive virtual gifts from viewers
- Gifts convert to diamonds, diamonds convert to cash
- Consistent live schedule builds live audience
- Best performing: Q&A sessions, listening parties, behind the scenes

TIKTOK SERIES:
- Paid content series — charge followers for exclusive multi-part content
- Best for: music tutorials, behind the scenes deep dives, industry knowledge

TIKTOK MUSIC:
- When your music is used in TikTok videos, royalties flow through your distributor
- More TikTok uses = more streaming royalties triggered
- Register music correctly with distributor for TikTok monetization

## Instagram Monetization

INSTAGRAM SUBSCRIPTIONS:
Requirements: 10,000 followers + creator account + eligible country
Revenue: $0.99-99.99/month subscriber fee, Instagram takes 30% (15% after first year)
Best for: exclusive content, early access, direct fan relationship

INSTAGRAM BADGES (Live):
- Viewers purchase badges during Instagram Live ($0.99, $1.99, $4.99)
- Artist receives approximately 55% after platform fees
- Consistent live schedule builds badge-purchasing audience

INSTAGRAM REELS BONUS:
- Invite-only program in some markets
- Pays bonuses for Reels performance
- Check Instagram Professional Dashboard for eligibility

INSTAGRAM SHOP:
- Sell merchandise directly through Instagram
- Tag products in posts and stories
- Integrates with Shopify and other platforms

## Patreon — Membership Monetization

TIER STRUCTURE BEST PRACTICES:
- $3-5/month: Early access to new music, exclusive updates
- $10-15/month: Behind the scenes content, monthly exclusive track
- $25-50/month: Monthly video call or Q&A, signed merchandise
- $100+/month: Personalized song, producer credit, direct relationship

CONTENT STRATEGY:
- Consistency is everything — members cancel when content stops
- Monthly minimum: one exclusive piece of content per tier
- Make members feel like insiders — not just subscribers

PATREON REVENUE POTENTIAL:
- 100 members at $10/month = $1,000/month recurring
- 500 members at $10/month = $5,000/month recurring
- Patreon takes 8-12% platform fee

## Twitch for Musicians

TWITCH AFFILIATE:
Requirements: 50 followers + 500 total minutes broadcast + 7 unique broadcast days + 3 average concurrent viewers
Revenue: Subscriptions ($4.99/$9.99/$24.99), bits, ad revenue

MUSIC ON TWITCH:
- Live studio sessions — show the production process
- Listening parties for new releases
- Collaborative songwriting sessions
- DJ sets and remixing
- Fan request sessions

Note: Twitch has complex music licensing — only play music you own or have licensed. DMCA issues can result in VOD mutes and account strikes.

## Bandcamp Monetization

- Direct music sales: artist keeps 85% after first $5,000 (then 90%)
- Merchandise: artist keeps 85%
- Subscriptions: recurring fan support
- Name your price: fans often pay above minimum
- Friday sales: Bandcamp waives fees on first Friday of month — promote heavily

VERSE (bookverse.com):

## NFTs and Web3 (Monitor Closely)

Market has contracted significantly from 2021-2022 peak but:
- Sound.xyz: music NFT platform with artist royalties
- Royal.io: fan ownership of royalty shares
- Foundation: digital art and music NFTs

Approach with caution — market is volatile and audience is niche. Monitor but do not prioritize over proven revenue streams.
