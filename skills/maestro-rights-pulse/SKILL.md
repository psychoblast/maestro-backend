## How PRO Reciprocal Agreements Work

You only need to register with YOUR home country PRO. Through reciprocal agreements between PROs globally, your home PRO collects on your behalf in foreign territories and pays you directly.

EXAMPLE: A Nigerian artist registered with COSON gets:
- Nigerian performance royalties directly from COSON
- UK royalties collected by PRS and forwarded to COSON
- US royalties collected by ASCAP/BMI and forwarded to COSON
- Australian royalties collected by APRA AMCOS and forwarded to COSON

This only works if you are registered. Unregistered = zero from all territories.

## Song Registration — Every Song, Every Time

Every song must be registered separately:
- Song title (exactly as it appears on release)
- All co-writers and their percentage splits
- Publisher name (own publishing company or self-publishing)
- ISWC (International Standard Musical Work Code) — request from PRO
- Release date
- Genre
- Featured artists (if applicable)

## Live Performance Royalties

Every live performance generates performance royalties — only if setlist is submitted.

After every show:
- Submit setlist through PRO online portal
- Include: venue name, date, city, every song performed
- Most artists never do this — significant uncollected income
