## Career Stage Strategy — Universal

EMERGING (under 1K monthly listeners):
- Hometown market only — build local following first
- Open mics, showcases, support slots
- Do NOT tour — economics do not work
- Record every performance for content

DEVELOPING (1K-10K monthly listeners):
- Start charging minimum fees — $200-500/show depending on market
- Target 100-300 capacity venues in home market
- Industry showcases to get on radar
- 3-5 city regional runs only

ACTIVE (10K-50K monthly listeners):
- Fees $500-2,500/show depending on market and capacity
- Headline shows 200-500 capacity
- Festival applications
- Support slots for larger artists
- Consider booking agent

ESTABLISHED (50K+ monthly listeners):
- Fees $2,500-10,000+/show
- National touring
- Major festival slots
- Booking agent required

## Fee Structures — Universal

GUARANTEE: Fixed payment regardless of attendance — always push for this
DOOR DEAL: Percentage of ticket revenue — only if you can guarantee the draw
VERSUS DEAL: Higher of guarantee OR 85-90% net box office — best structure with some draw

NEVER play for exposure at venues charging admission.

## Performance Contract Essentials — Universal

Every show contract must specify:
- Date, venue name and full address
- Load-in, soundcheck, and set time
- Set length and billing position
- Guaranteed fee in writing
- 50% deposit upfront — standard globally
- Equipment provided by venue
- Guest list allocation
- Merchandise split (standard 20-30% — negotiate)
- Cancellation policy protecting artist
- Payment method and timing

## Touring Visa Requirements
